The ICS Library is a room in the Institute of Computer Science which serves as the storage for different archives and references available to students and faculty members. These archives and references are special problem outputs, thesis, journal, DVD/CD, magazines, and other academic books. With the large amount of books and increasing number of study outputs available, manually keeping inventories and report generations of these resource materials are difficult and inefficient. In this project, a library system will be developed to improve the current practice in monitoring library resources and students availing the library services.
